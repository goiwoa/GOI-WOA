The datasets used in this study are derived from the classical Flexible Job Shop Scheduling Problem (FJSP) benchmarks. They are organized as follows:
Fattahi1.fjs-10.fjs: SFJS01-10
Fattahi11.fjs-20.fjs: MFJS01-10
MK01.fjs-10.fjs: MK01-10

Sources:
Brandimarte, P. Routing and scheduling in a flexible job shop by tabu search. Annals of Operations Research, 1993, 41(3): 157-183.
Fattahi, P., Saidi Mehrabad, M., & Jolai, F. Mathematical modeling and heuristic approaches to flexible job shop scheduling problems. Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing, 2007, 18: 331-342.
